Who am I ?

My name is Marie, I live in Rennes (France). Having always wanted to travel and discover other cultures, landscapes, ways of life, as soon as I had some savings, I used it to go abroad. I started to go to some places in Europe like Baleares and Canaries in Spain and then I went to other continents. I particularly enjoyed my experiences in Thailandia, and more recently, in Colombia. What I love with trips are meeting people from everywhere, face the unknown, live day by day and relativise my own culture and habits.

Bogota (Colombia), 2018
