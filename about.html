Who am I ?

Hello ! I'm Marie from France (Rennes), happy to see you there

Having always wanted to travel and discover other cultures, landscapes, ways of life, as soon as I had some savings, I used it to go abroad.

I started to go to some places in Europe like Baleares and Canaries in Spain and then I went to other continents. I particularly enjoyed my experiences in Thailand, and more recently, in Colombia.

What I love with trips are meeting people from everywhere, face the unknown, live day by day and relativise my own culture and habits.
