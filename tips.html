Let me share you some tips and advices

Be minimalist when travelling
►

The worse mistake we all (or almost) make when we travel for the first time is to bring a suitcase of 15 kg with a lots of "just in case". Forget it!
Buy first a 30L or, maximum, a 40L bag, and adapt the quantity of things to it, not the contrary. Like that, you'll feel really more free to move and you'll realize that you don't need a lot!

Get rid of the blisters
►

During my month of walk on the Camino de Compostella, I had many blisters after just a few days. I almost thougth about leaving, and juste next to the Hostel I meet my saviors : hiking sandals
I walked most of the trail with it, and it was just amazing, I didn't have any more problem after using it.

Travelling alone as a woman
►

Travelling alone car be scary for many people, and maybe especially for womens. But I think there are no more risks than everywhere, including at home. Don't listen to much stressful people and trust to your instinct and everything would be ok. Moreover, travelling alone is often the best way to meet people and challenge ourselves.

Be flexible !
►

Abroad, you will eat differently, people would act differently to you, etc. Keep in mind that everything, even what you judge "obvious" or "natural", can be influenced by culture. Sometime you will love it, sometime it will iritate you. Anyway try to stay open-minded and just receive what happens. This is the best opportunity to realize all that build us and how we could change it.
